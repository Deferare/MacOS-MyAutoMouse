# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** February 17, 2026

MyAutoMouse ("the App") respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect

The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit personal information.

## Permissions

The App may request macOS Accessibility permission so it can perform mouse automation features.

- This permission is used only to run app functionality on your device.
- No Accessibility data is uploaded to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

## Data Sharing

Because the App does not collect personal data, no personal data is sold or shared.

## Data Retention

The App does not retain personal data on external servers.

## Contact

For privacy questions, please open an issue at:

[https://github.com/Deferare/MacOS-MyAutoMouse/issues](https://github.com/Deferare/MacOS-MyAutoMouse/issues)

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated in the future. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
